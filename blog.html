OpenClaw AI

February 6th, 2026

Upon Reseraching AI, I was told to look into this called OpenClaw. OpenClaw is an open source LLM that you can locally host on your personal computer. It's main purpose is to serve you as your personal little assisstant that can not just only giving your answers to prompt but can actually run commands and task directly on your computer. OpenClaw is really cool but becareful with downloading it because giving root access of your computer to one single LLM can be a security nightmare.
